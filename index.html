ABOUT CRICKET SOCIAL

CricketSocial can help automate all aspects of a sports organization starting from application process, collecting payments, building team site, events & schedule management, collaboration and communication, cricket administration, squad management, data analytics, e-commerce, fan engagement, on-line scoring, live streaming and highlights. This will potentially increase sponsorships and uncover opportunities for your league players to represent higher level of cricket.
